What is it?

In a sea of things that need annotation, you would be lucky to find a Docfish. Docfish is exactly that: an annotation tool for experts to label things. It is an effort toward collaborative annotation to produce publicly available datasets for research. Docfish will (eventually) allow for a combination of human- and algorithm- derived annotations of text and images. This work is being done by Research Computing at Stanford University.
